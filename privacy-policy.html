Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • OAuth tokens and refresh tokens from Pinterest
  • Basic account info (e.g. user ID, profile image URL)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your Pinterest OAuth tokens solely to read and write Pins on your behalf.

3. Data Storage & Security

Tokens are stored encrypted in our database and are only accessible by our backend service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties apart from Pinterest as needed for API calls.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time by removing our app in your Pinterest account settings, or by contacting us at youremail@example.com.
